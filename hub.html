Online Services for you

We're making it easier and more secure for you to save with us.

What's new in Online Services?

Here are some of the key benefits you can expect from the new Online Services:

  • Instant deposits and withdrawals for faster access to your money
  • Regular, timely alerts and notifications to help protect your savings and keep you informed of important updates
  • Ability to open and manage your accounts digitally, anytime, anywhere
  • Real-time statements and earlier access to important documents, making it easier to stay on top of your finances
  • Easily and quickly update to your account and personal details without needing to contact us or waiting in long queues

What you need to do now

Please check your details are up to date:

  • Email address
  • Nominated Account number
  • Mobile number
  • Postal address

This will help you receive any important communications we send you. It will also help make your move to the new Online Services smoother, because we'll ask you to confirm your personal information when you register. Where we can, we'll send information about your account(s) by email, but we may still need to send some information by post. If any of your details are missing or incorrect, you can update them in one of the following ways:

Use your online account

Log in to online services and send us a secure message with your updated information. Select the 'Create message' option on the 'Messages' drop-down menu on your account's home page.

Or call us

Give us a call on 0345 122 1122, and we'll be happy to update this information for you.

We're available Monday – Thursday 08:00 – 18:00, Fridays 08:00 – 18:00, and Saturdays 09:00 – 16:00.

What happens next?

We will start the process by moving your Easy Access account(s) to the new Online Services later this year. Below is a timeline outlining when we'll be in touch with more information about your account, and any actions you may need to take.

JUL 2025

We send you an early notification to let you know about changes and what we need from you.

AUG 2025

You will receive detailed information about changes to your account(s). You can also review the documents further below in our Documents Hub.

SEP 2025

We'll share the next steps you need to take before your account(s) move.

OCT 2025

Your account(s) will move and you'll need to register for our New Online Services. You will need your address, email, mobile number and create new log in details (username/ password).
